# A&K Store

**Live:** https://akstor-e.netlify.app/
**Type:** E-commerce Web App
**Stack:** React · React Router · Context API · Cloudinary · Netlify

---

## Concept

A&K Store is a luxury fashion e-commerce platform built around a bold black-and-red identity. The premise is simple: fashion isn't just shopping — it's storytelling. Every collection is curated to carry a distinct mood, whether that's streetwear energy, minimalist elegance, or vintage nostalgia.

The design language reflects that ambition — high-contrast dark backgrounds, editorial photography, and sharp red accents that command attention without clutter.

---

## Sections

### Home

**Hero — "Elevate Your Styles."**
Split-screen opening: oversized headline left, full-bleed fashion model right. Two CTAs — *Start Shopping* and *Explore More*.

**New Collection**
Three-column product grid featuring the latest arrivals. Each card shows a product photo, name, price, and Add to Cart button.

**Our Partners**
Flip-card showcase of brand partners — Nike, Adidas, and Puma — with logos, descriptions, and brand quotes.

**Stay In Touch**
Bold newsletter signup with a single email field and a red CTA button.

---

### Shop

Filterable product catalog with tabs: **All · Men · Women · Kids: Boys · Kids: Girls**. Paginated grid (2 pages) of clothing, accessories, and watches with prices and cart actions.

---

### About

Brand story with an editorial split layout, followed by three trust pillars — **Wide Selection**, **Secure Shopping**, **Fast Delivery** — and a **Customer Feedback** section with star-rated testimonials.

---

### Contact

Contact details (email, phone, Salé, Morocco) with a name / email / message form and a **Send Message** CTA.

---

## Features

- Dark-first design with red accent `#DC1E28`
- Category filtering on the shop page
- Cart with live item count in the navbar
- Dark / light mode toggle
- Cloudinary-hosted product images
- Fully responsive
- Deployed on Netlify
